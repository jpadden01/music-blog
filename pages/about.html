About

My name is James and this is website is a journal of my taste in music as well as a channel for practicing my website making skills.

About Me

I am a commputer science student who enjoys coding, listening to music, and talking about music.

My experience with coding is limited to Java and Python. I have mostly made CLI applications and games with simple graphics. This website is my first personal project involving web development.

I don't feel opposed to many (if any) genres of music, but I feel like my music taste and playlists are very limited. I listen to the same few albums very frequently, and go through phases of liking a new album or artist where they get added to, or even take over my rotation, but only temporarily. I don't listen to as much new music as I used to, and I definitely don't listen as attentively. I'm excited to change that.

About the Website

I hope this website will fix two problems:

  1. I need a place to practice web development
  2. I want to broaden my musical taste

I think that lectures and assignments are important while learning, but that practice is just as beneficial. Having criteria leads to developing websites that are boring, and mindless to make. I hope that by having no structure or reference for this website, I can learn to apply the skills I am learning in a more realistic scenario.

I love to listen to music but with school, work, and having friends, I don't give music the attention it deserves. TV shows, movies, and games are fun activities to do with friends. They can also only be enjoyed during my spare time, unlike music. The fact that I can listen to music so often, ironically, makes me listen to less music. Having a project that involes listening to music will hopefully let me attribute some importance to one of my favourite activities.
